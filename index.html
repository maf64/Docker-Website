Introduction

This webpage will educate a new user about Docker, and how it compares to virtuliaztion.

Over recent years Docker has become very prevalent in the tech world. Each tab above will give you more information.

- The Virtualization tab will introduce you to the subject.
- The Containerization tab will introduce you to th subject.
- The Comparison tab will compare Virtualization and Containerization
- The Tutorial tab will show you how to setup a webserver on your local computer using Docker.
- The Commands tab will show you basic Linux commands and what they do as well as how to log into your Docker Container.
- The Connect tab will show you how to connect Docker with Pycharm to write python code.
